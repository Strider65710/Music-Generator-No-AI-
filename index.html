Project .jis files & save codes

A .jis file holds your whole project (sequence, sections, sound, tracks, edits). Or copy a short save code to paste anywhere:

Syntax: letters = sections · [CD] together · A*4 repeat · (AB)*2 repeat a group · - bridge · | quick fill · . rest bar · ! stop · ~ resolve. Drag chips to reorder. Space play · V/B/E tools.
